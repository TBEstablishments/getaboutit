▸ ABOUT GO

Go — Board Game

Place stones on intersections. Surround opponent stones to capture them. Most territory at the end wins. Played on 9×9 here for shorter games.

How to play

  • Click an empty intersection to place
  • Pass button when stuck
  • Score auto-counted at end of game

Tips

  • Corners are stronger than sides; sides stronger than centre
  • Don't play into atari (one liberty left)
  • Two eyes = alive forever
