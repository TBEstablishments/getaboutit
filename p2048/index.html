▸ ABOUT 2048

2048 — Puzzle Game

Slide tiles into matching pairs. Two 2s merge into a 4, two 4s into an 8, all the way up to 2048 (and beyond). The board only adds new tiles — never removes them — so every move is a planning move.

How to play

  • Arrow keys to slide
  • Swipe on mobile
  • Every slide spawns a new 2 or 4

Tips

  • Pick a corner and never abandon it
  • Keep your highest tile locked there
  • Plan three moves ahead before you slide
