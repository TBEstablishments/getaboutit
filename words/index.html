Words — Word Game

Six attempts to find a five-letter word. After each guess, letters glow green (right letter, right spot), yellow (right letter, wrong spot), or dark (not in the word).

How to play

  • Type letters
  • Backspace to delete
  • Enter to submit

Tips

  • Start with vowel-heavy words like ADIEU or AUDIO
  • Don't repeat eliminated letters
  • Save tricky letters (Q, J, Z) for later rounds
