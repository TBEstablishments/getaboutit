▸ ABOUT CONNECT 4

Connect 4 — Board Game

Drop a disc into a column; it falls to the bottom of that column. Four in a row in any direction wins. Centre column is the strongest opening.

How to play

  • Click or tap a column to drop
  • Hover to preview
  • New game button after each round

Tips

  • Open with the centre column
  • Watch for double threats — two ways to win at once
  • Never let the opponent claim the centre
