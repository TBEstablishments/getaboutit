▸ ABOUT FLAP

Flap — Arcade Game

Tap to flap. Don't hit the pipes. Don't hit the ground. The gap stays the same; your nerve does not.

How to play

  • Tap, click, or press space to flap
  • Each tap = one upward impulse
  • Gravity does the rest

Tips

  • Tap rhythmically — small flaps beat big ones
  • Look at the next gap, not the bird
  • First three pipes are slow; pace yourself
