▸ ABOUT BLOCKS

Blocks — Puzzle Game

Falling-blocks at its purest. Seven shapes drop one at a time — rotate, slide, lock. Fill a row to clear it. Lose when the stack reaches the top.

How to play

  • Arrows: left/right to move, down to soft-drop, up to rotate
  • Space for hard-drop
  • Mobile: tap-zones on the sides + a centre rotate

Tips

  • Keep a clean well for the long bar
  • Multi-line clears score exponentially
  • Hard-drop is faster than waiting
