▸ ABOUT PONG

Pong — Arcade Game

The original. Two paddles, a square ball, and a long volley. First to eleven wins. The AI ramps up as the score climbs.

How to play

  • Mouse/trackpad up and down
  • Arrow keys also work
  • Tap and drag on mobile

Tips

  • Hit the ball on the moving edge of your paddle to angle it
  • Predict where the ball will arrive — don't chase
  • Centre yourself between shots
