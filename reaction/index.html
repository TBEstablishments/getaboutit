▸ ABOUT REACTION

Reaction — Reflex Game

Wait until the screen turns green, then tap. Tap too early and the timer resets. Average human reflex is ~200ms; pro gamers hit ~150.

How to play

  • Click or tap anywhere
  • Don't tap during the red/yellow phase
  • Five tries averaged into your final score

Tips

  • Look at the centre — peripheral colour shifts are slower
  • Hover your finger close to the screen
  • Caffeine helps; sleep helps more
