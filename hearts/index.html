▸ ABOUT HEARTS

Hearts — Card Game

Four players, 13 tricks. Hearts and the Queen of Spades cost points; everything else is free. Lowest score wins. Take ALL the hearts and the Queen to "shoot the moon".

How to play

  • Click a card to play it
  • Must follow suit if you can
  • Pass three cards left/right/across each hand

Tips

  • Get rid of high spades before the Queen comes out
  • Dump high hearts on tricks you can't avoid
  • Shoot the moon is high-risk, high-reward
