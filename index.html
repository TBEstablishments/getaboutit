QUESTIONS

Is GETABOUTIT really free?

Yes. All 16 games are completely free to play. No signup, no ads, no in-app purchases.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Just click a game and play. Your scores save locally in your browser.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Every game is built mobile-first with touch controls. Add to your home screen for a full-screen experience.

How often are new games added?

The arcade is curated, not constantly expanded. Quality over quantity. Daily challenges rotate among the existing 16 games.

Does it track me or sell my data?

No tracking. No analytics. No data collection. All your game data lives only in your browser's local storage.

Can I play offline?

Once you've loaded a game, it stays playable as long as the tab is open. We don't currently support full offline mode.
