▸ ABOUT SNAKE

Snake — Arcade Game

Snake started life on a Nokia keypad in 1997 and never left. The rules are dead simple: eat a dot, grow longer, don't cross yourself or hit a wall. The fun is watching a tidy snake become an unmanageable kebab.

How to play

  • Arrow keys or WASD to turn
  • Swipe on mobile
  • Don't cross your own tail or hit a wall

Tips

  • Cut corners early to avoid late traps
  • When you fill the screen, start spiralling inwards
  • Speed ramps up as you grow
