▸ ABOUT BLACKJACK

Blackjack — Card Game

Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Aces are 1 or 11; face cards are 10. Dealer hits on 16, stands on 17.

How to play

  • Hit / Stand / Double / Split / Insurance buttons
  • Place bet then deal
  • Splits allowed up to four hands

Tips

  • Always split aces and 8s; never split 10s
  • Double on 11 unless the dealer has an ace
  • Basic strategy beats every casino chart — memorise it
