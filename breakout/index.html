Breakout — Arcade Game

1976's answer to Pong-with-bricks. Bounce a ball off a paddle to break a wall of coloured rows. Each row has a different point value. Clear the wall to advance.

How to play

  • Mouse / trackpad to move the paddle
  • Arrow keys also work
  • Launch with click or space

Tips

  • Hit the ball near the paddle's edges to angle it
  • The ball speeds up after you break the top rows
  • Don't panic when it falls — track ahead
