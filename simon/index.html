▸ ABOUT SIMON

Simon — Memory Game

Four coloured pads. Simon lights them in sequence; you repeat. Each round adds one more colour. Miss and you lose.

How to play

  • Tap a pad to play it
  • Watch then repeat
  • Each round is one longer than the last

Tips

  • Say the sequence out loud as it plays
  • Chunk longer sequences into groups of three
  • Most humans top out around 16-18 in sequence
