# BRAND.md — getaboutit in one page

## What it is
The free arcade. 34 games. No signup. No tracking. No ads. Forever.

## The vibe
1985 arcade reborn for 2026 browser. Vaporwave outrun, chromatic
aberration, scanlines, Press Start 2P. The constraint is the brand.

## Wordmark — GETABOUTIT
Always uppercase. Always chromatic: white center + `#ff006e` pink shifted
right + `#00f5ff` cyan shifted left. Breathes at 0.125Hz on the home page.

## Palette — 10 colors, never compromise
`#ff006e` pink · `#d100d1` magenta · `#8338ec` purple · `#3a0ca3` deep purple
`#4361ee` blue · `#00f5ff` cyan · `#06ffa5` teal · `#ffd60a` yellow
`#ff9500` orange · `#ef233c` red
Plus `#0a0a1e` bg, `#ffffff` sparingly. Yellow is the celebration color.

## Font — Press Start 2P
Wordmark, headings, scores, labels. Body copy in system monospace.

## Five signature moves
1. The breathing wordmark
2. Per-game color identity (34 owners)
3. The deterministic pixel signature (Ticket + Card + leaderboards)
4. The reactive horizon
5. The CRT power-on ceremony

## The three modes
THE FLOOR · THE CABINET · THE LOBBY

## What we will never do
No accounts (just Tickets). No avatars. No real-time. No ads. No
tracking. No emoji as icons. No audio packs. No trading. No engagement
metrics on you.
