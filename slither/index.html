▸ ABOUT SLITHER

Slither — Arcade Game

Snake reimagined as an arena. Eat glowing dots to grow. Don't touch another snake. Other snakes dying becomes dinner for everyone else.

How to play

  • Mouse / finger to steer
  • Click and hold to dash (costs length)
  • Wraparound arena edge

Tips

  • Circle a smaller snake to box it in
  • Don't dash unless you're escaping
  • Big food clusters appear where snakes died — feast carefully
