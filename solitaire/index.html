▸ ABOUT SOLITAIRE

Solitaire — Card Game

Klondike — the solitaire your grandmother played. Seven tableau columns, four foundation piles by suit, a stock to draw from. Build foundations Ace to King.

How to play

  • Drag cards between piles
  • Double-click sends to foundation if legal
  • Undo and auto-complete in the toolbar

Tips

  • Empty a column for King-stacking
  • Don't flip stock cards unless you need to
  • Many seeds are unwinnable — accept losses
