▸ ABOUT POKER

Video Poker — Card Game

Five cards dealt. Choose which to hold; the rest re-deal. Final hand pays per the Jacks-or-Better paytable. Royal flush triggers the progressive jackpot.

How to play

  • Click a card to toggle Hold
  • Deal/Draw button
  • Bet 1-5 button (5 unlocks the royal multiplier)

Tips

  • Always hold a pair of Jacks or higher
  • Four to a flush beats four to a straight
  • Bet max for the royal flush multiplier
