▸ ABOUT DOTS

Dots and Boxes — Puzzle Game

Take turns drawing one edge between two dots. Complete the fourth edge of any box and you claim it. Most boxes wins.

How to play

  • Click or tap between two dots to draw an edge
  • Completing a box gives an extra turn
  • Game ends when no edges remain

Tips

  • Avoid drawing the third edge of any box — it gives the opponent a free box
  • Force chains: late game, give them the smallest possible chain
  • Sacrifice short chains to win long ones
