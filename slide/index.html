Slide — Puzzle Game

A 4×4 grid with one empty slot. Slide a neighbouring tile into the empty slot. Solve when 1-15 are in order with the empty slot bottom-right.

How to play

  • Click or tap a tile next to the empty slot
  • Arrow keys also work
  • Move counter at the top

Tips

  • Solve the top row first, then left column
  • The bottom 2×3 is a single piece — never break it
  • Daily seed gives the same shuffle to everyone
