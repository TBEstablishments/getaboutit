▸ ABOUT MINES

Minesweeper — Puzzle Game

Reveal all the cells that aren't mines. Numbered cells tell you how many mines touch them. Logic gets you most of the way; the last few cells are educated guesses.

How to play

  • Left-click to reveal
  • Right-click (or long-press) to flag
  • First click is always safe

Tips

  • Flag everything you're sure of — it gates safe moves
  • When stuck, look for corners with a single unknown neighbour
  • The last 50/50 guess is a coin flip; accept it
