▸ ABOUT CHESS

Chess — Board Game

Full chess with all rules: castling, en passant, promotion, threefold repetition. The AI uses minimax search with a piece-square evaluation table.

How to play

  • Click a piece, then click its destination
  • Drag also works
  • Undo and difficulty buttons in the toolbar

Tips

  • Castle early — get your king safe
  • Don't move the same piece twice in the opening
  • Easy AI plays 2 ply; Hard plays 4-5 ply
