▸ ABOUT CHECKERS

Checkers — Board Game

Move diagonally forward. Jump enemy pieces to capture them. Reach the far row to king a piece — kings move both directions. Captures are mandatory.

How to play

  • Click a piece, click its destination
  • Multi-jumps chain automatically
  • Kings are crowned visually

Tips

  • Trade favourably — three for three is fine when you're ahead
  • Force the opponent into double-jumps
  • Keep your back row intact early
