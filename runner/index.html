▸ ABOUT RUNNER

Runner — Arcade Game

You're always running. Tap to jump. Hold to jump higher. Boost when the gauge is full. Don't fall.

How to play

  • Tap, click, or space to jump
  • Hold for variable jump height
  • Double-tap for boost when available

Tips

  • Short jumps for small gaps; long jumps cost more
  • Boost cuts through long obstacle chains
  • The world speeds up — pace yourself
