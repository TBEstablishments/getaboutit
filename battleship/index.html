▸ ABOUT BATTLESHIP

Battleship — Board Game

Place five ships on a 10×10 grid. Take turns calling shots at the opponent's grid. First to sink all five wins. The AI hunts in the same way you do.

How to play

  • Drag ships to place; click to rotate
  • Tap a cell to fire
  • Hit/miss/sunk feedback after each shot

Tips

  • Spread your ships — don't cluster
  • When you hit, fire adjacent cells
  • Big ships are easier to find; place them out of the centre
