▸ ABOUT BACKGAMMON

Backgammon — Board Game

Race your 15 checkers around the board and bear them off your home. Dice rolls dictate moves. Hit an opponent's single checker and it goes to the bar — they must re-enter before doing anything else.

How to play

  • Roll button (or auto-roll)
  • Drag a checker the dice distance
  • Doubles let you play four moves

Tips

  • Make points — stack two checkers on a single triangle to deny the opponent
  • Don't leave singles in danger zones
  • Race position: prioritise speed; back-game: prioritise hits
