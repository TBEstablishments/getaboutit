▸ ABOUT LIGHTS OUT

Lights Out — Puzzle Game

Tap a cell and it flips itself and its four neighbours. Turn every light off. Sounds simple. Isn't.

How to play

  • Click or tap any cell
  • Toggles cell + N/S/E/W neighbours
  • Solvable in ≤15 moves on every seed

Tips

  • Solve top row first by chasing lights downward
  • The last row tells you how to fix the first one
  • Symmetric boards have symmetric solutions
