Tic Tac Toe — Board Game

Three in a row wins. Centre is strongest; corners are next. The AI plays perfectly — best result is a draw.

How to play

  • Click or tap any empty cell
  • You play X, AI plays O
  • New game button after each round

Tips

  • Take the centre first if it's open
  • Force two threats at once to win
  • Against perfect play, draw is the win
