Invaders — Arcade Game

Lines of aliens shuffle left, right, and down. You shuffle along the bottom row and fire upward. They speed up as their numbers thin.

How to play

  • Arrows or A/D to move
  • Space or up to fire
  • Bunkers absorb both their shots and yours

Tips

  • Shoot the bottom row first — they descend on you
  • Save bunkers for the late game
  • The last invader is always the fastest
