▸ ABOUT TYPE RACE

Type Race — Typing Game

A passage of text and an AI opponent. Type the passage as fast as you can. WPM and accuracy update live. Cross 100 WPM and the screen warps.

How to play

  • Type the visible text
  • Errors block until corrected
  • Race ends when the passage is complete

Tips

  • Accuracy first — backspacing costs more than slow typing
  • Look at the next two words, not the current letter
  • Touch-type the spacebar — it's a quarter of your keystrokes
