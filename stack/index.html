    ▸ ABOUT STACK
    

      
Stack — Arcade Game

      
Stack is the one-tap tower. Each block slides across the screen — tap to lock it. Imperfect timing trims the next block smaller. Climb high enough and the world cycles through neon, space, and the void above the sky.

      
How to play

      
    
      
  • Tap, click, or press space to drop the block
    • 
      
  • Time perfectly to keep the block full-width
    • 
      
  • Each floor cycles the colour palette
    • 
      

      
Tips

      
    
      
  • Watch the leading edge, not the centre
    • 
      
  • When the block gets tiny, slow down — half a heartbeat counts
    • 
      
  • Daily seed: same sequence for everyone that day
    •
